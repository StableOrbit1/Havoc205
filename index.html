MEGS 2026 Youth By-Laws

Official rule guidelines for the U13 Division.

U13 Specific

Game Duration & Roster

  • Total Innings: Games are scheduled for 6 innings.
  • Minimum Players & Late Arrivals: A team must have a minimum of 7 players to field a team and start the game. If a player shows up late, they must be added to the bottom of the batting order (bottom of the inning).
  • Continuous Batting: All players on the roster must bat in a continuous order.
  • Mandatory Play (Sitting): Coaches must ensure fair defensive rotation. No player may sit out more than 2 innings per game.

Field Dimensions

  • Pitching Distance: The pitching rubber is set at 38 feet for the U13 division.
  • Base Paths: Bases are set at 55 feet (standard U13) or 60 feet depending on field configuration.

Base Running

  • Leading Off: Runners cannot take a lead until the pitched ball crosses home plate.
  • Advancing on a Walk: In one continuous motion, a batter-runner who receives a walk may advance to and attempt to take 2nd base. This is permitted even if the pitcher has the ball inside the pitching circle.

Mercy Rules

  • Per Inning Limits: Innings 1 through 4 are capped at a maximum of 5 runs per team. Innings 5 and 6 are capped at a maximum of 8 runs per team.
  • Game Mercy (15 Runs): The game is called if a team is leading by 15 runs after 3 or more full innings.
  • Game Mercy (10 Runs): The game is called if a team is leading by 10 runs after 4 or more full innings (or 3½ innings if the home team is ahead).

Pitching Restrictions

  • Inning Limits: Pitchers are restricted to a maximum of 3 innings per game to protect their arms.
  • Definition: Delivering one single pitch in an inning constitutes a full inning pitched for the purpose of this rule.
  • Hit Batters: If a pitcher hits 3 batters in a game, they must be removed from the pitching position immediately.
